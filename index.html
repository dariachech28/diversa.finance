What Diversa is

One flagship on-chain yield product.

Diversa is designed as a simple one-click on-chain yield product for users who want DeFi returns without managing DeFi themselves.

The core design is a canonical vault model with a primary home chain, transparent reporting, and a controlled operational process rather than yield chasing or complexity for its own sake.

Positioning

On-chain yield strategy platform.

Diversa is not positioned as a bank-like savings product and not framed as a traditional discretionary fund.

The product is intended to provide transparent access to diversified on-chain yield opportunities through a clean product experience.

Home chain

Arbitrum

The canonical vault model is centered on Arbitrum, chosen for a balance of DeFi depth, EVM maturity, cost efficiency, and operational simplicity.

Core asset

USDC

The platform is designed around canonical USDC for accounting, settlement, and portfolio reporting.

Product model

Single flagship vault

The launch direction is one primary strategy and one clear user experience, with simplicity and trust prioritized over fragmentation.

How it is intended to work

Clear structure, controlled operations, transparent reporting.

01

Flexible entry

Users are expected to be able to enter from multiple chains and assets, with flows routed under the hood into canonical USDC on Arbitrum.

02

Vault shares

The product is structured as a vault-first model where users receive shares representing proportional exposure to portfolio NAV.

03

Weekly NAV and redemptions

The initial operating design is based on weekly NAV publication and weekly redemption processing, aligned with a lean two-person team.
